Current site behaviour

This first version does not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, user accounts or an online contact database. The eligibility screen runs in your browser. Answers are not sent to us or stored by the site.

Email contact

If you contact us by email, your email provider and ours process the message. We use the information to answer the request, organise a requested service and maintain necessary business records.
